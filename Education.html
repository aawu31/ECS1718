Education

This is some to all of the education requirements to be a zoologist. Specific degrees and divisions such as a specific job desired are mentioned below.

Ecologist

  1. Bachelor's Degree in Ecology

  2. *Master's Degree in Ecology

  3. *Doctorate in Ecology

* Recommended for Independent Research

Geneticist

  1. Bachelor's Degree in Biology

  2. *Doctorate in Biology

*May be required for research positions.

Paleontologist

  1. Bachelor Degree in Geology or Biology

  2. Masters Degree in Paleontology

  3. *Doctorate

*Most companies require this to be in a position offered.
