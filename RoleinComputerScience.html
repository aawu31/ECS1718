Impact

The impact of being a zoologist helps revolutionize the world by finding ways to help the environment and the animals that live there. You get to be one-on-one with animals that are in need of love and attention. Constant discoveries are made each day within the field of zoology. If you become a zoologist, you can contribute to the discoveries that improve the lives of millions of species.

Role in Computer Science

Zoology has a large part in the world of computer science. Zoologists rely on the internet to obtain new information and spread their ideas. Mass amounts of data are available for zoologists via technology. They also use computers to record data. New technology has made equipment more accessible for zoologists.
